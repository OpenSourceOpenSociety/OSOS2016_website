The internet has fundamentally rewritten the rules for human culture. We can transparently exchange information on a scale we have never been able to imagine before.

We can work together and collaborate at a species level.
Our individual freedom to innovate has never, ever been greater.

We have the collective potential to radically improve the world so it works better for everyone. Right now.

Welcome to the age of active participation.

Welcome to the world where being open is better for business, technology and democracy.

Welcome to #OSOS 2016

Join a community of active global citizens building a more open world.


We are an open community, this conference provides an opportunity for us to work together to answer/ get clearer on the following questions...
How can we employ open principles to drive innovation and growth in our workplace culture?
Who is leading the world in open, innovative business culture?
What are the big opportunities for new business in the open source world?
How open is New Zealand really?
What emerging open source technology is on the horizon?
What barriers can we remove to enable more open source technology in New Zealand?
How can open data help our decision makers?
Can open data and journalism change how democracy works?
How can we use the internet to enable more people to engage with decisions that affect all of us?
How far away is an internet enabled democratic system?
How could an internet based democracy work in New Zealand?
