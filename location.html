Welcome to Wellington - the city known as the "coolest little capital"

Set against a beautiful waterfront Wellington is famous for its vibrant arts scene, world class caf√© and restaurant culture, and active outdoor lifestyle. Wellington enjoys more cafes, bars and restaurants than New York City, and its coffee and craft beer producers are internationally recognised. Exploring the city and visiting attractions is easy with most central city locations within easy walking distance and supported by a comprehensive local bus network.

For more information about Wellington and local attractions please visit www.wellingtonnz.com

Car Parking

Michael Fowler Centre - parking for approximately 88 car parks with three disability spaces is available at the Michael Fowler Centre car park. Parking charges are $4 per hour Mon-Fri 6am-6pm and $2 per hour on Friday from 6pm. Weekend parking is free.

TSB Bank Arena Parking - At the TSB Bank Arena, public and exhibitor parking facilities are available at Wilson Parking located beneath the venue. For further information on hours and costs, please contact Wilson Parking.

St James Theatre - Wilsons Parking have covered and open air options available at 84 Tory Street and 221 Wakefield Street.

Note: Given the easy accessibility of Michael Fowler Centre by foot or the local bus network parking at your accommodation and walking / busing in may be more cost effective.

Airport Travel

Travel to the central city by taxi from the Wellington Domestic or International airport terminals will cost approximately NZ$35. Wellington Taxis can accept cash in New Zealand dollars and credit card payments

The Wellington Airport Flyer bus services transports visitors and luggage from the airport terminals to the central city for $9 New Zealand dollars. The Airport Flyers leave every 10 minutes and offer Free Wifi. Cash only.
