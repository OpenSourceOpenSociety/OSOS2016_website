Car Parking

Michael Fowler Centre - parking for approximately 88 car parks with three disability spaces is available at the Michael Fowler Centre car park. Parking charges are $4 per hour Mon-Fri 6am-6pm and $2 per hour on Friday from 6pm. Weekend parking is free.

TSB Bank Arena Parking - At the TSB Bank Arena, public and exhibitor parking facilities are available at Wilson Parking located beneath the venue. For further information on hours and costs, please contact Wilson Parking.

St James Theatre - Wilsons Parking have covered and open air options available at 84 Tory Street and 221 Wakefield Street.

Note: Given the easy accessibility of Michael Fowler Centre by foot or the local bus network parking at your accommodation and walking / busing in may be more cost effective.
