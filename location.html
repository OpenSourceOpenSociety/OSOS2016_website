Location

Wellington, known as "The coolest little capital in the world" is New Zealand’s centre of government and the world’s southernmost capital city. It is also New Zealand’s cultural capital. Vibrant and exciting, Wellington combines the stimulation and sophistication of a big city, with the quirkiness of a small harbour town with a thriving arts and cultural scene, superb cafes and restaurants, all in a stunning harbour side setting. Wellington is said to have more bars and restaurants than New York and is also considered the craft beer capital courtesy of a trail of specialist brew bars and a population with a palette for quality over quantity. Visit www.wellingtonnz.com for more information.
