Is it a tech conference for tech people?

Nope. OS//OS is not just a tech conference. This is a conference about how open technology impacts society.


Is it an open source conference?

Not in the normal way. We are championing open principles like participation, collaboration, transparency and freedom to innovate. We are working to make the potential of open source technology more accessible and understood across wider audiences — but we aren’t strictly an open source technology conference.

Who comes to OS//OS?

OS//OS brings together people from across business, government, public agencies, city, community and technology sectors. Journalists, software developers, policy analysts, researchers, managers, business developers, team leaders, product managers, data analysts, consultants, entrepreneurs, activists and investors...

The event attracts people looking to sharpen their skills, learn from the best and connect with other innovators.
