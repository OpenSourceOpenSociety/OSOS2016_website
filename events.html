It’s a conference?

Yes! But not just your standard conference. OS//OS is about action, discovery and putting big ideas and potential into practical real world outtakes.

Expect to be be inspired by leaders in their field, expect to get hands on and practical learning new skills in workshops, expect open spaces to connect, collaborate and put the power of open, active participation and collaboration into action.


Who is OS//OS for?

OS//OS is for innovators. People who want to understand how open principles can change the world and their work.

  • Technologists.
  • Business owners.
  • Team leads.
  • Managers.
  • Policy commentators and analysts.
  • Journalists.
  • Policy researchers and writers.
  • Consultants.
  • Entrprenuers, activisits and community interested in how open principles can improve.







What’s the programme?

Join us for 2 days of epic professional development, sharpen your skills by learning from the best and connect with over 400 innovators from across the business, government, technology and digital sectors.
